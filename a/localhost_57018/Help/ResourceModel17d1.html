PassportStatus

Defines the available status for a given passport

Possible enumeration values:

NameValueDescription
Active

0

Passport is allowed to dispense from a machine, assuming no other restriction rules
Disabled

1

Passport is temporarily restricted from dispensing from machines
Deleted

2

Passport is permanently restricted from dispensing from machines, and no longer visible on the VendNovation portal. Historical transactions are still available
