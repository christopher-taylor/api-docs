OrganizationType

Indicates organization type

Possible enumeration values:

NameValueDescription
Manager

0

Manager Organizations manage machine configuration and inventory and can assist in managing a clients passports.
Client

1

Clients organizations manage their own users and restrictions, but do not directly manage machine configuration or inventory. Clients are tied to Managers through an account.
Corp

2

Corporation organizations have reporting access to account level data from multiple client type organizations for higher level reporting.
